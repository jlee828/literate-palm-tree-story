There once was a palm tree named Paul. This palm tree possessed a special quality. What is that quality you might ask? Paul, the palm tree was literate. Yes, Paul could read.
How did Paul learn to read? Well that is where our story begins...
Paul once saw an open book on the beach.He found it interesting and began to learn to read.
Paul wanted to read! So one day, Paul evolved four feet. Paul can walk!
Paul went to the beach and began to read the whole book.
Paul learned to read!
